Food Calories Estimation

Estimating the calorie content of food using computer vision and machine learning.

About the Project

The Food Calories Estimation project uses machine learning and computer vision to accurately estimate the calorie content of food based on images. By analyzing features such as food type, portion size, and other visual characteristics, the system provides an estimate of the food's caloric value. This solution can assist users in maintaining a balanced diet by offering quick, on-the-spot calorie estimations! It’s an innovative approach to helping individuals track their food intake more effectively.

The project leverages deep learning models trained on large datasets of food images, which are processed to classify different food items and calculate their calories. With the help of convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and other advanced image recognition techniques, it can make calorie estimation more accurate and accessible for everyone.
